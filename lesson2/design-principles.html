White Space

Featuring Apple

Link to site

Apple has always been known for their simple sleek design in both their hardware and their software. Their website is no exception and the reason that they are able to do this is largely becasue they try to have as little thing on the screen as possible and give each item it's own space. This can be seen on their website with one simple large image and only a little bit a text with a lot of extra space.

Visual Hierarchy

Featuring Amazon

Link to site

Amazon is really good at using the design principle of Visual Hierarchy in order to help you to see what they want you to see at a glimpse while allowing you to look deeper at further content if you are interested. They do this by keeping the navigation bar large at the top, making the image of the product for sale the largest thing on the screen, and also making the buy button large. This way it is easy for customers to buy at a glance, without getting distrated by a bunch of extra content unless the user is looking for it.
