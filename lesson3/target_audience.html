Home Target Audience Design

Target Audience

Primarily local Valley County business owners and representatives looking to network and connect with other businesses in the area, access resources and support for their operations, and have a voice in community and government affairs. The website may also attract potential investors and entrepreneurs, as well as community members seeking information on local events and resources

5 use case scenarios website use

  • A local business owner, Mary, wants to find out about networking events and other opportunities to connect with other businesses in the area. She visits the chamber's website and finds a calendar of upcoming events, as well as information on how to become a member.
  • Parents of a small family looking to move to Valley County visits the site to see what job oppertunities would be available to them if they move there.
  • A new resident, Tom, wants to learn more about the local economy and business climate in Valley County. He visits the chamber's website and finds a section with statistics on population growth, median income, and other key indicators.
  • A tourist, Jane, wants to find out about things to do and see in Valley County. She visits the chamber's website and finds a list of local attractions, as well as a directory of local hotels and restaurants.
  • A business owner, Sarah, wants to learn more about the chamber's member benefits and how they can help her business. She visits the chamber's website and finds a section outlining the benefits of membership, such as access to networking events and discounts on products and services.
